Abilities

Passive

Transcedence

Xavier enhances his subsequent skills each time his skills hits an enemy hero. Stage I: Increases Movement Speed by 50% when subsequent skills hit, decaying over 1.2s. Stage II: Width of subsequent skills will be greatly increased. Stage III: Skill Cooldown reduced by 4s ( 12s for the Ultimate ). At Stage III, Xavier enters the Transcedence state.


Transcedence: All of Xavier's skills are enhanced to Stage III state for 5s ( this duration is extended by 1s each time Xavier's skill hits an enemy ).

Infinite Extension

CD: 6.5, Mana Cost: 75

Xavier fires a Mystic Bullet that deals damage to enemies it passes through. The Bullet's flying distance increases each time it hits an enemy or the Mystic Barrier

Skill II

Mystic Barrier

CD: 14.0, Mana Cost: 100

Xavier conjures a Mystic Barrier that lasts 5 seconds. Enemies that come into contact with the Barrier will take 125–250 and be slowed by 50%, while allies will gain extra Movement Speed that decays over 1.2 seconds.When hit by Xavier's other skills, the Barrier will expand into a forbidden field for 3 seconds, dealing 300–600 (+120% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies within and immobilizing them for 1.2 seconds.
